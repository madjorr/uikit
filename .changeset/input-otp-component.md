Add `InputOTP` — a bare row of single-character boxes for entering a one-time code, with auto-advance between slots, Backspace-to-previous, and paste-to-fill-all support. `length` controls the slot count (default 6); `error`/`disabled`/`autoFocus`/`onComplete` round out the API. Themed by the `--ui-input-otp-*` token tier.
