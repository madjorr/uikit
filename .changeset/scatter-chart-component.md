Add `ScatterChart` — a typed recharts composition over the shared `Chart` primitives. Plots a `series` list (each with its own point array) on numeric `xKey` / `yKey` axes, with an optional `zKey` for bubble sizing, a `shape` marker prop, and a themed tooltip/legend/axes/grid with caller-supplied series colors. No CVA variants (scatter's expressiveness is in the data mapping). Initial version ported from the apps/demo `ScatterChartPlayground`; design + data-viz palette reconciliation pending.
